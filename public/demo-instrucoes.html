Step-by-step for the reviewer:
  • 1. Authenticate the app as Scanner Demo (click here to authenticate).
  • 2. To test adding points: scan the "Add Points to Demo Loyalty Card" QR Code. No account creation or adding the card to the Wallet is required.
  • 3. To test redeeming a reward/coupon: scan the "Redeem Demo Coupon/Reward" QR Code.
  • The loyalty card and coupon are for demonstration only. The demo flow is fully isolated and does not affect real data. You can repeat the test as many times as you wish.
